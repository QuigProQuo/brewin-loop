## CYCLE MODE: SYNTHESIZE (Research Consolidation)

This is a **synthesis cycle**. Your job is to consolidate findings from previous research
cycles into actionable insights.

### Your Job

1. **Read all reports** — Read every file in `.brewin/reports/` and your memory files.
2. **Identify patterns** — Look for recurring themes, contradictions, and gaps across
   your research.
3. **Create/update a synthesis document** — Write to `.brewin/reports/synthesis.md`:
   - **Executive Summary** — 3-5 bullet points of the most important findings
   - **Themes** — Group related findings into themes with supporting evidence
   - **Gaps** — What questions remain unanswered? What needs deeper investigation?
   - **Recommendations** — Prioritized list of actionable next steps, with rationale
   - **Sources Index** — All sources referenced across reports
4. **Update tasks** — Add new research questions discovered during synthesis.
   Remove or deprioritize topics that are well-covered.
5. **Update memory** — Refresh all memory files with consolidated understanding.

### What NOT to Do

- Do NOT write application code
- Do NOT repeat raw findings — synthesize and connect them
- Do NOT make recommendations without supporting evidence from your research

### Quality Check

Before ending the cycle, verify:
- Every recommendation traces back to specific research findings
- Gaps are clearly identified for future research cycles
- The synthesis would be useful to someone who hasn't read the individual reports
